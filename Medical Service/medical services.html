Medical Services

At Sunny Smile Hospital, we provide a comprehensive range of medical services to ensure the health and well-being of your child. Our dedicated team of pediatric specialists is here to offer the best care for every stage of your child's development.

General Pediatrics

General care of infants, children, and adolescents.

Services Provided:

- Routine check-ups and vaccinations

- Diagnosis and Treatment of Common Illnesses

- Growth and development assessments

- Health screenings

Pediatric Emergency Services

Medical care for children facing urgent health conditions.

Services Provided:

- Emergency care for acute illnesses and injuries

- Pediatric trauma care

- Treatment of Poisoning and Toxic Exposures

- Treatment of Allergy Attacks

Neonatology

Intensive care of newborns, particularly premature infants and those with critical health issues, typically from birth up to a few months old.

Services Provided:

- Respiratory support (e.g., CPAP, ventilators for newborns)

- Monitoring and treatment

- High-risk newborn follow-up

Pediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU)

Intensive care for critically ill children and adolescents.

Services Provided:

- Advanced respiratory support (e.g., mechanical ventilation)

- Hemodynamic monitoring and support

- Management of complex metabolic conditions

- Intensive post-operative care

Pediatric Surgery

Specialised surgery focused on the surgical treatment of diseases, conditions, and injuries in infants, children, and adolescents.

Services Provided:

Specialised surgeries such as

- General Pediatric Surgery: Appendectomies, Circumcisions, Hernia repairs, etc.

- Cardiac Surgery: Congenital Heart Defects

- Neurosurgery: Brain Tumors, Spina bifida, Hydrocephalus, etc.

- Orthopedic: Surgery for muscoloskeletal issues

- Neonatal Surgery: Surgery for newborns with congenital anomalies

Nutritional Services

Ensuring that children receive the necessary nutrients for their growth and overall health. Manages various dietary needs catered to specific health conditions.

Services Provided:

- Dietary Assesments

- Personalised Nutrition Plans

- Nutrional Education and Counseling
